Customer Care - Booking Issues

⚠

Issue: Unable to Select Seat

If you are unable to select a seat during the booking process, please try the following solutions:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies, then refresh the page.
  • Make sure you have selected a train before attempting to select a seat.
  • Try using a different web browser or device to see if the issue persists.
  • Contact our customer support for further assistance.
⛔

Issue: Error Message during Booking

If you encounter an error message while booking, please try the following solutions:

  • Double-check that you have entered the correct information, such as passenger details and payment information.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection and try again.
  • If the issue persists, wait for a few minutes and try again later.
  • If the problem continues, contact our customer support for assistance.
🛈

Issue: Ticket Not Received

If you have not received your ticket after booking, please follow these steps:

  • Check your email inbox, including the spam or junk folder, for the ticket confirmation email.
  • If you cannot find the email, make sure you provided the correct email address during the booking process.
  • If you still cannot locate the ticket, contact our customer support with your booking details for further assistance.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

How do I cancel tickets on railways?

Please follow these simple steps to cancel your ticket on railways: - Log in to railways - Visit the booking section on the app - Select the desired booking, scroll down and click on the 'cancel ticket' tab to initiate cancellation. - In case of partial cancellation, please select the desired passenger(s) and click on the cancel tab.

How do I track refund on railways?

Please follow the steps mentioned below to track your refund status: 1. Log in to your railways account 2. Click on the customer service tab 3. Select your trip from the booking section 4. Click on the booking ID and check the refund status

Train Reservation Cancellation Guidelines

Before proceeding with the cancellation, please read and follow the guidelines below:

  1. Cancellations are subject to the cancellation policy of the respective train service provider.
  2. Cancellation charges may apply based on the time of cancellation and the type of ticket purchased.
  3. Please provide accurate PNR number and ticket number for cancellation.
  4. Ensure that the captcha code entered is correct for verification purposes.
  5. Once the cancellation is processed, the refund will be initiated as per the refund policy of the train service provider.
  6. Please note that some tickets may be non-refundable or have specific cancellation restrictions.
  7. For any queries or assistance regarding ticket cancellation, please contact our customer support.
